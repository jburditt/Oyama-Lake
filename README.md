# 6. Generator

## Check the diesel tank level
- there is a tube on the right of the tank that indicates remaining diesel level
  
## Filling the diesel tank
- reverse truck with diesel tank to the stairs in front of diesel generator
- take blue gas knozzle to diesel tank
- unscrew cap on right top side of tank
- put the knozzle in hole on top right where cap was
- put the empty canister on top right shelf in the knozzle lever to force it always on
- clamp the red booster to red cable on top of diesel tank in truck
- clamp the black booster to black cable
- clamp the red on other side of cable to the red terminal on battery inside diesel shed
- clamp the black 
- verify diesel is flowing by lifting knozzle slightly
- if no diesel is flowing, move the blue hose around and check for kinks 
- when the truck diesel tank pump sounds different, unhook everything 
- you can also check how much diesel is left by lifting up the tank

## Return tank
- reverse truck to the table in front of the shop
- lift the tank using the pump metal parts and lift over the tailgate in small increments
- once over the tail gate, you can push the tank to the table and put the knozzle on top of the tank
